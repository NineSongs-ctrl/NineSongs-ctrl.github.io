People over profits

Green Website

Mike Schreiner

Age: 53

Healthcare
  • Recruit 3,500 doctors and connect all Ontarians to primary care within 4 years
  • Increase nursing student enrolment by 10% per year (aiming for 2,500 extra nurses by 2030)
  • Reduce surgery wait times via centralized referral system
  • Build new hospitals in Huntsville and Bracebridge
  • Legislate 10 sick days for all employees
  • Cover all mental health & addiction costs under OHIP
  • Build 48,000 non-profit long-term care homes
Housing
  • Build 2 million new homes over the next 10 years
  • Construct 310,000 affordable non-profit/co-op homes (including 60,000 supportive units)
  • Develop 22,000 affordable homes by Indigenous communities
  • Allow fourplexes province-wide and permit six-unit buildings on transit corridors
  • Offer interest-free loans for building affordable rental units
  • Remove development charges for homes under 2,000 sqft & waive Land Transfer Tax for first-time buyers
  • Introduce rent control & strengthen eviction protections
Affordability
  • Phase in a universal basic income plan
  • Double ODSP/welfare rates and offer tax cuts for incomes under $65K
Education
  • Increase per-student funding by $1,500
  • Establish an independent review of the school funding formula
  • Cap classroom sizes at 24 (grades 4–8) & 26 for kindergarten
  • Double the number of students in school meal programs
  • Eliminate EQAO testing
  • Waive tuition for select in-demand programs & convert student loans to grants
Public Safety
  • Enhance community policing & improve emergency response systems
Environment
  • Safeguard 30% of natural areas by 2030
  • Create an Ontario Foodbelt and expand it to include a Bluebelt for waterways
  • Provide EV purchase rebates & remove HST on energy retrofits
Transportation
  • Expand GO Transit service and inter-regional bus networks
  • Create dedicated truck lanes on Highway 407 & remove tolls for commercial vehicles
Economy
  • Support local green jobs & invest in renewable energy projects
Groceries
  • Enforce anti-price gouging regulations & support community-owned food markets
Social Support
  • Implement a universal basic income & increase funding for social services
